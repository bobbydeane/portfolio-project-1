For more than three decades the “The Troubles” had an immense impact on the residents of Belfast. Few were impacted by the violence, and every community has their own tale to tell. Now you can go beyond the history books and investigate the real stories with first hand accounts from those intimately involved. Explore the world’s largest outdoor art gallery as you examine the many murals, and get the opportunity to sign the Peace Wall and leave your thoughts alongside those of Bill Clinton and the Dalai Lama.

Go beyond the official guide books and explore the city through the history of the political conflict know as “The Troubles”. Explore the communities on both sides of the Peace Line with the woman and men who were imprisoned for their cause. Hear from both Loyalist and Republican communities about the conflict and its aftermath and learn how the political murals tell a tale of history and culture of the area from a unique perspective. Discover how the city has transformed from those dark days.
